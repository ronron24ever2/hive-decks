Skilled operators are getting harder to find.

02 / 10   THE MODEL

Three things the rest of the market cannot offer together.

Autonomy vendors either bolt hardware onto your machines, lock you into a single OEM, or build for US-only fleets. Hive is the only operator running a remote operations centre model with zero physical changes to your existing Volvo equipment.

01 · RETROFIT-FREE

Your Volvo. Unchanged. Still under OEM warranty.

Hive uses the sensors, the CAN bus and the telemetry your L-series already has. No rack bolted to the roof. No electronics in the engine bay. The machine you bought is the machine you keep.

vs. Gravis / bolt-on racks: hardware on every machine, every time.
02 · OEM-AGNOSTIC PLATFORM

Built around a fleet you already own.

Volvo L60 through L350 (2018+) is supported today. The operations model — not the hardware — is the product. When we extend to additional platforms, your operators do not learn a new tool.

vs. Cat Command / Komatsu FrontRunner: only inside the OEM's own catalogue.
03 · ONE CENTRE, MANY SITES

Supervised from Kristiansand, Norway.

One trained operator supervises several machines at once from a single European operations centre. Your people stay on the site doing what they do best. The keyboard-and-joystick hours move to a facility built for them.

vs. Teleo / Bedrock: US-centric, still scaling their European presence.
